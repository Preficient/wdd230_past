Target-audience

The target audience is those business or company owners who wish to belong to a contact network where they can meet other owners and perhaps future suppliers.
People linked to or interested in the business field, and who wish to increase their knowledge and network of contacts, or learn more about the city's businesses.

Personas:

Juan:
He is a entrepreneur of 45 years. He has a consolidated textile company in Lima. He is looking to take an export course in order to expand his business to other countries.

María:
María is a housewife, she is 28 years old. She is starting a small jewelry business. She is looking for vendors in her city. Since day by day her production is growing.

Scenarios:

  • Anyone interested in taking a course on business management
  • Own business owner looking for a supplier
  • Increase network of contacts
  • Get to know companies present in the city
  • Find tax or business advice
